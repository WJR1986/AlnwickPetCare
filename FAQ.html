FAQs

What services do you offer?
At Alnwick Pet Care, we offer a wide range of pet care services tailored to meet the needs of your furry companions. Our services include dog walking, pet sitting and overnight stays.

What areas do you serve?
We proudly serve pet owners in Alnwick and surrounding areas.

How do I book your services?
Booking our services is easy! You can schedule pet care appointments with us through our website contact form or by giving us a call at 07488315367.

What are your rates and payment options?
Our rates for pet care services vary depending on the type of service, location and the duration. For detailed pricing information, please contact us directly. We accept payments via cash or bank transfer.

Are you insured and bonded?
Yes, we are fully insured to ensure the safety and security of your pets and property.

What are your cancellation and refund policies?
We understand that plans can change. Please refer to our cancellation and refund policies on our website for detailed information on how we handle cancellations and rescheduling.

Do you offer group or individual walks?
We offer one-to-one, individual dog walks for dogs from the same household or social circle. Group walks are a great way for social dogs to interact, while individual walks cater to pets who prefer one-on-one attention.

How do you handle emergencies or medical situations?
Your pet's safety is our top priority. In case of emergencies or medical issues, we have established protocols and contact information for local veterinarians to ensure your pet's well-being.

What is your availability?
We are available everyday and also offer services on weekends and holidays for your convenience.

Can I schedule recurring appointments?
Yes, you can schedule recurring appointments with us to provide consistent and reliable care for your pets. Please let us know your preferred schedule, and we will accommodate your needs.

How do you ensure the safety of my pet?
We maintain strict safety measures, including leash policies, secure transportation, and a commitment to client information confidentiality, to ensure your pet's safety at all times.

What should I do if my pet has specific dietary or medical needs?
If your pet has specific dietary or medical requirements, please inform us during the booking process, and we will ensure those needs are met.

What if my pet doesn't get along with other animals?
We understand that not all pets are social butterflies. If your pet doesn't get along with other animals, please let us know, and we will take appropriate measures to ensure their comfort and safety.
