Services

Dog Walking (One-on-One)
I offer dog walking on a one-on-one basis or with dogs from the same household. I believe this is safer for the dogs, which is the most important aspect of the services I provide.

This personalised attention enables me to provide a service both you and your dogs deserve.

Walks start from £12 an hour in Alnwick, with fuel charges applied to those areas further afield. This service is fully flexible so don't be scared to ask!
Pet Sitting
I offer pet sitting services at your home to minimise disruption and distress for your pets.

I believe most pets, especially cats and dogs, do not like being left in boarding away from home. At home they have their familiar comforts and scents to reduce anxiety and stress.

Pet sitting starts from £12 an hour in Alnwick, with fuel charges applied to those areas further afield. Again, this service is fully flexible so don't be scared to ask!
